About Me

Hi there! I'm an EECS Master's student at UC Berkeley. I am passionate about using AI and engineering tools (software, hardware, ML..) to solve problems in translational healthcare and neuroscience.

My research focuses on personalized multimodal health AI, brain-computer interfaces (BCI), and robotics.

Some of my non-academic interests are basketball, climbing, video games, spending time with my cats, reading, cooking, and traveling.
